Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beatten eggs
coocked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Preparation time

Total: Approximately 10 minutos.

Preparation: 5 minutes.

Cooking: 5 minutes.

Ingredients

2, 3 Eggs.

Salt, to taste.

Pepper, to taste.

01 Tablespoon of butter or oil.

Optional filligns: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs.

Instructions

01. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

02. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

03. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the
pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

04. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.

05. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.

06. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
